📋 What data we collect

If you use HireEU, we collect:

Account Information

Email address Your email (required for login & recovery)
Password Hashed with bcryptjs (never stored in plaintext)
Name Optional — you can leave it blank
Created date When you created your account

CV & Profile Data

CV file Extracted text from your uploaded PDF or DOCX (used for parsing only)
Career summary Professional overview extracted from your CV
Primary role Your job title / desired position
Skills Technical tools, languages, frameworks (extracted via AI)
Years of experience Total work experience (0–70 years)
Seniority level Junior, mid, senior, or lead
Location Your city/country (optional)
Languages Languages you speak (from CV)

Job Search Preferences

Preferred location Cities/countries you're interested in
Work type Remote, hybrid, onsite, or any
Minimum fit % Lowest match score you'll accept (0–100)
English only Whether to show only English-language jobs

What we DON'T collect: Phone number, IP address, device fingerprinting, cookies (except session auth), browsing history, or location data beyond what you volunteer.

🎯 Why we collect it

Account data To let you log in, reset your password, and keep your profile safe
CV & profile To parse your CV, extract skills, and match you with relevant jobs
Search preferences To filter jobs by your location, work type, and fit threshold

We do not:

  • Sell your data to recruiters, job boards, or anyone else
  • Use your email for marketing or newsletters (unless you opt in separately)
  • Share your CV with employers without your explicit consent
  • Train AI models on your CV data

⚙️ How we use your data

CV Parsing: When you upload your CV, we extract text and send it to Groq API (Claude Llama 3.3 70B) to parse your profile (skills, experience, seniority). The CV text is used for this parsing only and is stored for future reference — you can delete it anytime.

Job Matching: We use your profile (skills, years of experience, seniority) to score jobs from our aggregators (JSearch, Adzuna, Polish job boards). We compute a match percentage (0–100%) and rank results. Your data stays on our servers during this process.

Cover Letter Generation: When you request a cover letter, we send your profile summary + the job description to Groq API to generate personalized text. The cover letter is not stored — it's generated on-demand.

Email Communication: We send transactional emails (password reset, account notifications) via Gmail SMTP. We do not send marketing emails unless you explicitly opt in.

🔗 Third parties & integrations

HireEU uses the following services. Your data flows to them only as necessary for HireEU to function:

Groq API (AI)

Processes CV text and generates cover letters. Groq's privacy policy.

Neon (Database)

Stores user accounts, profiles, and preferences on PostgreSQL. Neon's privacy policy.

JSearch API via RapidAPI

Provides job listings globally. Search queries are sent to RapidAPI. RapidAPI's privacy policy.

Adzuna API

Provides EU job listings. Adzuna's privacy policy.

Railway (Scraper)

Hosts a custom scraper for Polish job boards (JustJoinIT, NoFluffJobs, EnglishJobs.pl). No personal data is sent. Railway's privacy policy.

Gmail SMTP (Email)

Sends transactional emails. Google's privacy policy.

Important: We use these services to make HireEU work. Their privacy policies apply to data they receive. We do not add extra processing or delays — your data is used only as needed.

🌍 Where your data is stored

Primary storage: Neon PostgreSQL (Frankfurt, Germany — EU)

Your account, profile, and preferences are stored in an EU data center and encrypted in transit.

Temporary processing: When you upload a CV or generate a cover letter, the data is sent to Groq API servers (US-based) for AI processing. The results are returned and stored locally on Neon.

EU Compliance: Data stored in the EU is subject to GDPR. Data sent to Groq (US) is covered by Standard Contractual Clauses.

🔒 How your data is protected

Passwords Hashed with bcryptjs (10-round salt); never stored in plaintext
Authentication JWT tokens (7-day expiry) for session management
Database Neon uses SSL/TLS encryption (requires HTTPS)
API communication All API calls use HTTPS
Data isolation Each user's data is isolated by UUID; no cross-user leaks

We don't: Use cookies for tracking, store session tokens in localStorage (only secure HTTP-only cookies), or log sensitive data.

⚖️ Your rights under GDPR

If you're in the EU or EEA, you have the right to:

Access Request a copy of all data we hold about you
Correction Ask us to fix incorrect data (e.g., wrong email)
Deletion Ask us to delete your account and all data (right to be forgotten)
Portability Get your data in a portable, machine-readable format
Objection Object to processing for marketing (we don't market anyway)

To exercise these rights, email hireeuapp@gmail.com with the subject "GDPR Request: [access/deletion/correction/portability]". We'll respond within 30 days.

🗑️ How to delete your account

You can request account deletion at any time. Here's what happens:

Send an email to: hireeuapp@gmail.com

Subject: "Delete my HireEU account"

We will:

  • Delete your account, password, and profile
  • Delete your CV data and search preferences
  • Confirm deletion within 48 hours

We won't: Recover deleted data once you confirm. This is permanent.

Alternatively, if you're logged in, you can delete your account directly in the app settings (coming soon).

📊 Logging & data retention

Server logs: Vercel (our hosting provider) logs basic request data (IP, method, status) for debugging. These are deleted after 30 days.

Your data: We keep your account and profile data as long as your account is active. After deletion, we retain no backups or copies (we're a small project, not a big corporation with tape backups).

Job search history: We don't track or store your job search history. Matches are computed fresh each time.
